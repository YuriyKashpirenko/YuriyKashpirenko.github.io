Apple Watch Hermes

Artistry unbound.

A partnership based on parallel thinking, singular vision and mutual regard continues with the latest Apple Watch Hermès collection. Featuring built-in cellular technology, new handcrafted leather bands from Hermès, and an additional Apple-designed watch face inspired by the classic Hermès Carrick font, it’s the ultimate tool for modern life.

Single Tour Rallye

Tapping into a storied history in the world of automobile racing, this new band references the design of the classic Hermès driving glove. Available in Noir Gala leather, paired with a 42mm stainless steel case.

Single Tour Éperon d’Or

Timeless and elegant. The printed pattern on this meticulously handcrafted band is based on a legendary Hermès scarf design.

Single Tour

The classic Hermès design. The buckle recalls those on the straps of a horse’s girth, a nod to the equestrian heritage of Hermès. Available in Fauve Barenia leather paired with 38mm and 42mm stainless steel cases, and Indigo Swift leather with a 42mm stainless steel case. Bands in additional colours available separately.
